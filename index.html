Things to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of power outlets are available in Taniti?

The power outlets in Taniti are 120 volts similar to the outlets in the United States

What time can alcohol be served or sold?

In Taniti alcohol cannot be sold or served between the hours of midnight and 9:00 am.

What is the drinking age in Taniti?

Although not strictly enforced the official drinking age is 18 years old.

Is English spoken in Taniti?

Fluent english is spoken by many younger Tanitians. Older residents and people in the rural areas speak very little english.

Are there hospitals in Taniti?

Taniti has several clinics and one hospital. The hospital employees speak multiple languages.

Is Taniti Safe?

Taniti is very safe with violent crime being extremely low.

Are there National Holiday that result in closures?

Yes, Taniti has a large number of national holiday which result in tourist attractions and restaurant closures.
